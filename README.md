# Ten Interpretations, One Muse

A minimal, interactive digital gallery exploring how beauty is perceived through different artistic lenses.

## Concept

This project began with something personal.

I took a photograph of someone I deeply admire and imagined how history’s most iconic painters might respond to her presence — not by describing her literally, but by expressing how she feels to them.

Each artist “speaks” in their own voice, offering a short, intimate reflection shaped by their philosophy, style, and way of seeing the world.
